Janitorial Cleaning

We believe that janitorial services play a crucial roll in preventing the spread of illnesses and are the key for maintaining a clean and healthy living environment. Regular disinfection of high touch areas and the cleaning of shared spaces can considerably reduce the risk of infections. This can give residents a peace of mind knowing that they are living in a safe, clean, and sanitized environment.

Pressure Washing

Our company provides high-pressure water spray services to remove loose paint, mold, grime, mud, and dirt from surfaces. It can protect your home from long term damage and keep your space looking spotless.

Leaf Blowing

During the fall season we offer the service of collecting loose leaves and debris in the surrounding areas of a building. We use leaf blowers, rakes and brooms to clear driveways and main entrances. In some areas due to by-law requirements, we avoid using machines due to noise issues and instead use other alternatives that get the job done while remaining quiet. It is important to remove the leaves to avoid trapped moisture that can create problems with fungus, mold, and diseases.

Junk Removal

Our junk removal service guarantees that your unwanted items will be removed from your property. Whether you are decluttering, relocating, redecorating, or renovating, you will benefit greatly from junk removal. The surroundings of your building or house will look clean and presentable.

Pool Operations

We provided the services of a Certified Pool Operator (CPO) designated by Fraser Health Authority. This will ensure that the pool is well maintain applying regulating safety standards such as sanitary and safety conditions.

Graffiti Remvoal

Graffiti is an act of vandalism that indicates urban depreciation and damage to property values. We offer in our service's different methods of graffiti removal.

Painting

The painting service is offered to improve the appearance of the inside and outside of a building and to protect it from damage or deterioration of water, corrosion, insects, and mould.

Tile & Grout

We offer the tile and grout cleaning service to remove dirt and stains from the surface and joints of tiles. We use heat-based technology, cleaning solutions, vacuum power, and high-pressure rinse to remove dirt and grime. We complete the job by applying a sealer that protects against mildew and staining. It will give a clean and better appearance to your building.
