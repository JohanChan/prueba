BUILDING MAINTAINANCE

Mission

Our main goal is to provide customers with a clean, safe, and healthy environment following high standards in our services. It is important for us to build long-term relationships with our clients to better understand their needs and provide exceptional service while also implementing maintenance solutions to improve our clientele's facilities.

Vision

Become the most trusted customer satisfaction company in British Columbia and make a positive impact in our community by applying Optimum Star's main values.

  • Safety and Sustainability- Safeguarding the health and well being of our clients, our staff, and the environment.
  • Trusting long-term relationships- Treating everyone with dignity and respect while building strong connections with clients and staff.
  • Attention to detail- With Optimum Star we have highly trained workers who pay attention to detail to make sure nothing is left unclean.
  • Responsible and Reliable-We make sure to provide the appropriate equipment for our workers to make sure everything is in top condition.

History

Optimum Star is a building maintenance company owned by Rodolfo Contreras that delivers exceptional customer service, trusting relationships with clients and staff and follows thorough safety procedures. The company was founded in 1993 by Markus Fankhauser. He always had a positive attitude and was quick to respond to the client's requests. Through his hard work and dedication, the company has grown significantly. As a building maintenance company, we have been through different experiences such as the Covid-19 pandemic. It was a difficult time because most of the businesses in Vancouver were temporarily closed but our company continued uninterruptedly servicing our existing customers. Despite all the changes we persevered by accommodating and responding promptly to each building's needs. This made a positive impact which solidified our existing contracts and opened doors to new contracts. Present day we continue to grow and be a part of the everyday lives of our clients by delivering satisfaction through the services we provide.
